Purpose

I created this site not only as a personal reference or virtual , but also as a way to develop new skills. While I had used HTML and CSS in coursework, I had not had the chance to experiment using Bootstrap. I knew Bootstrap was a powerful toolkit for designing responsive and professional-looking websites, but I didn't quite know where to start.

By following online tutorials, watching YouTube videos, and scouring forums, I was able to build what you see before you. Although similar results could be achieved with a low code/no code alternative such as Weebly or Squarespace, it was important for me to take the opportunity to develop my web-based coding skills. Even uploading HTML files to a domain was a new experience for me.

I hope you will take the time to explore the pages I have created and learn more about who I am. Feel free to contact me with any questions or inquiries.
